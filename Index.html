4 Who We're Reaching

Different patients need different approaches. Here are three types we target — each with their own engagement strategy.

New Patient
The Researcher
Has a condition or pain point. Actively searching online. Comparing providers, reading reviews, checking credentials. Hasn't contacted anyone yet.
How we engage
  • SEO content for their searches
  • Google & social ads
  • Landing page → consultation form
  • AI voice/SMS follow-up in minutes
  • Email nurture if not ready yet
Referred Patient
The Referral
Sent by their PCP or another provider. Has intent but still validates online — checks your website, reads reviews, looks at social profiles before calling.
How we engage
  • Strong review profile (Google, Healthgrades)
  • Professional website & provider pages
  • Active social media presence
  • Referring physician email updates
  • SMS appointment reminders
Existing Lead
The Undecided
Visited your site, downloaded a guide, or started a form — but didn't book. Or: a past patient who hasn't returned for a recommended follow-up.
How we engage
  • Email re-engagement sequences
  • SMS/voice check-in outreach
  • Retargeting ads on social & web
  • Educational social content
  • Patient success stories & reviews

No single channel converts alone. Here's what real patient paths look like when everything is coordinated.

The multiplier effect

Each channel reinforces the others. SEO builds awareness. Ads re-engage. Email nurtures. Social builds trust. AI voice/SMS prevents missed leads. Reviews seal the decision. Together, they create a path no single channel can achieve alone.
